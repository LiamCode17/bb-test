• No aggressive or antisocial behaviour will be tolerated. All dogs must undergo a behavioural assessment (1 - 2 hours, free of charge) prior to their first visit.
  • Owners must provide a completed Enrolment Form and signed Liability Waiver prior to, or upon their dog(s) first visit.
  • We reserve the right to refuse or expel any dog.
  • We do not allow power breeds such as Pittbulls, Rottweilers, German Shepherds, Boerboels, Bull Terriers, Staffordshire Terriers, Doberman Pinschers, Rhodesian Ridgebacks and Huskies.
  • Dogs must be a minimum age of 6 months, with initial vaccinations done.
  • All dogs must be in good health.
  • All dogs must be vaccinated against Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus, Bordetella and Rabies.
  • All dogs must be neutered or spayed.
  • All dogs must be on regular tick, flea and deworming programs.
  • All dogs must arrive on a leash.
  • All dogs must be microchipped and fitted with a nametag with their owners contact details on it.
  • Payment is due upon receipt of an invoice. Regular clients will be invoiced at the end of each calendar month.
