About Billy Bob's

Billy Bob’s Dog Resort is situated at Pypies Square Park in Devil’s Peak – one of the Mother City’s most beloved dog hangouts. We provide a fun, healthy and safe home away from home with all the love, comfort, exercise and stimulation your pooch deserves. Our activities range from exhilarating walks to action packed neighbourhood patrols and play at the park with the local dogs. We also have a garden to relax and play in, and there are loads of toys, chews, beds, couches and sunny spots to choose from, as well as a cosy fireplace during winter! There’s no denying, at Billy Bob’s…

Our Services

Hourly Care

Available 7 days a week
  • Day (7am-5pm): R35 per hour
  • Evening (5pm-11pm): R55 per hour
  • We provide hourly care for up to 5 hours. Thereafter the full daycare rate applies.

DAYCARE

Available 7 days a week 7am-7pm
  • Irregular: R200 Per Day
  • 1 Day per week: R185 per day
  • 2 Days per week: R170 per day
  • 3 Days per week: R160 per day
  • 4 Days per week: R150 per day
  • 5-7 Days per week: R140 per day
  • We feed the dogs here at 7am and at 6pm. Owners must please feed their dogs prior to arrival, and provide food for dinner if necessary. Alternatively, we provide meals (pellets and / or wet food) at R30 per meal.
  • We charge R35 per hour for daycare dogs collected after 7pm.

Sleepovers

Available 7 days a week
  • 1 Night: R290 per night
  • 2 Nights: R270 per night
  • 3 Nights: R255 per night
  • 4 Nights: R235 per night
  • 5 Nights or more: R220 per night
  • Rates are charged in 24-hour periods from the time of arrival. A R35 per hour charge applies to additional hours on the last day.
